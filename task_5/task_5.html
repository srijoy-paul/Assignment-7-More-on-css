Google Fonts

Google Fonts is a free and widely-used web font service provided by Google. It offers a diverse collection of high-quality fonts that can be easily integrated into web projects. With a simple link or CSS inclusion, developers can enhance the typography of their websites by choosing from a variety of fonts, including serif, sans-serif, display, and handwriting styles. Google Fonts provides a user-friendly interface, allowing easy selection, customization, and implementation of fonts to improve the visual appeal and readability of web content.
